Welcome to the Sci-Fi Portal

Thank you for signing up to our community!

Have you ever read a work of science fiction so spectacular that you feel the galaxy needs to know about it?

Then this is the place for you! As a member of the Portal you can see reviews of these masterpieces by fellow 'Portalers' at our books page. You can also share your own journeys into the world of Sci-fi by easily submitting your own reviews. You can also upload an image of the book!

You can easily add to, edit or delete reviews that you add to the site. You can become more involved in the community by voicing your opinions on reviews by other Portalers using the comments section at the bottom of each individual review. Other users can also comment on your posts but don't worry, although we love a discussion within the Portal, it is a safe space and comments must be approved by the admin team!

When you signed up to the Portal we automatically created a profile for you, so feel free to add to, or edit this, if you want to let us know a little more about yourself!

Don't forget to check out our authors page to see a list of some of our favourite authors. We are always adding more so keep an eye out for new inspiration. You won't be disappointed! If you have an author who you think we need to hear about, drop us an email and one of the team will get to work on adding their bio to the Portal!

One final piece of advice before you set off into the Portal: did you know you can use our handy search feature to search for books and authors directly from the home page? Be sure to check that out too!

Welcome again, and we hope you enjoy your journey in the Portal!
